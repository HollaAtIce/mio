I co-founded The DIME and led it from inception to completion. The DIME was a video classifieds app for buying, selling and promotion. Every ad is a video of 30 seconds or less, recorded from the camera on your smartphone. I wrote our business plan, created the app workflow, found and managed developers, raised seed money and learned to get stuff done. Watch a video about of it here.

The DIME was an iOS app built using Objective-C and Amazon Web Services.
